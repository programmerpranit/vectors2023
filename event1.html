CAMPUS CALLING

Round 1:-

General Aptitude exam via google forms. Participating students will get a link for the exam via their mail Id’s or whatsapp message. A specific time will be allotted to complete the aptitude exam and later an average score will be calculated to enter the next round.

Round 2:-

In the second round the students who have cleared the aptitude exam will face a group discussion with other students on a common topic provoded by the judges. The judges will assess your group discussion performance and reward you points. This round Will be conducted on Google-Meet platform.

Round 3:-

In the final round, the shortlisted students will have to face a personal interview with the judges, the judges will assess you knowledge and accordingly reward you with some points. The highest point scorer, 1st place student will be rewarded a cash prize and an online certificate, and followed by the 2nd place student and the 3rd place students too. This round will be conducted on Google-Meet platform.
